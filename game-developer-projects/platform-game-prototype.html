Some Features of the Game

Stars Orbs Gameplay Mechanics

Stars orbs- get 1 star to open the gate and advance to the end of the level. You can get up to a maximum of 3 star on each level. A certain number of stars are required to open new levels.

Hight Score Gameplay Mechanics

Hight score- a score is creating at the end of the play level based of the time and coins amount.

Moving Platforms Gameplay Mechanics

Moving Platforms- These platforms move back and forth in a specified area.

Coin’s Collection Gameplay Mechanics

Coin’s collection- there is coins around d the level for the player to collect.
